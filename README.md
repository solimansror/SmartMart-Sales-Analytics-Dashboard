# SmartMart Sales Analytics Dashboard 📊🛒

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze SmartMart sales performance across multiple business dimensions.

The dashboard focuses on revenue tracking, profitability analysis, sales representative performance, quantity analysis, and customer purchasing behavior over time.

---

## 🚨 Business Problem

Retail businesses must continuously monitor sales operations to improve profitability and operational efficiency.

However, raw transactional data alone cannot clearly answer important business questions such as:

- Which sales representatives generate the highest sales?
- When do customers buy the most?
- Which days drive stronger revenue?
- How efficient is profitability performance?
- What is the impact of returned transactions?

This dashboard transforms sales data into actionable business intelligence.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze total sales performance.
- Measure total profit and profit margin.
- Evaluate sales representative contribution.
- Monitor quantity sold patterns.
- Analyze customer purchase timing.
- Understand return transaction behavior.
- Support business performance optimization.

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### Sales Performance Analysis
- Total Sales monitoring
- Profit analysis
- Profit Margin evaluation
- Quantity tracking

### Sales Representative Analysis
- Revenue contribution by sales representative
- Performance comparison

### Time Analysis
- Sales by Time of Day
- Sales by Day of Week
- Monthly and yearly performance trends

### Return Analysis
- Returned vs Non-returned transactions
- Sales quality monitoring

## 🔍 Key Insights

- SmartMart achieved **3.81M total sales**.
- Profit reached approximately **808K**.
- The business maintained a **21.25% profit margin**.
- Thursday showed the highest sales performance.
- Sales activity varies across different periods of the day.
- Sales representatives contribute differently to overall revenue.
- Return analysis provides visibility into transaction quality.
